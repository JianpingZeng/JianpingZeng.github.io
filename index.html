Biography

I am a PhD student advised by Dr.Changhee Jung in the department of Computer Science of Purdue University. Prior to joining Purdue University, I worked as a compiler engineer and senior software engineer in Alibaba Group with a Master degree from the School of Computer Science of Huazhong University of Science and Technology.

Research

My research interests lie on computer architecture, e.g., intermittent computing, nonvolatile memory, and crash consistency etc, and compiler optimizations for underlying new architectural features.
